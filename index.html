Our Start

Since 2016 we are engaged in the field of learning development by providing learning experiences at palestinian level and training mobility opportunities for young people, adults and teachers.

Our focus is on investing on expertise, creativity and enthusiasm to shape a knowledge ecosystem, based on the development of skills and competences for the benefit of individuals, public institutions, civil society organisations and public society as a whole, finally contributing to growth and ensuring equity, prosperity and social inclusion in palestine and beyond.

For knowledge Academy, the cooperation with other companies operating in the field of education,programming is vital for a sustainable impact in the students future.

Knowledge academy solved some of the biggest challenges facing students and prepare them for the labor market mission

Our Concept

Knowledge Academy gives the oportunity for any person to learn coding with our proffional team. We want to create a world where anyone can build something meaningful with technology, and everyone has the learning tools, resources, and opportunities to do so. Code contains a world of possibilities — all that’s required is the curiosity and drive to learn. At knowledge academy, we are committed to empowering all people, regardless of where they are in their coding journeys, to continue to learn, grow, and make an impact on the world around them.
